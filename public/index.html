Meet Our Founder
Outnovately AI was founded by Temitope Okeseeyin
A Top 1% Global HR-Tech Strategist, AI Consultant, and Workforce Development Expert. With 10+ years of experience on both sides of the hiring table - as a recruiter, coach, consultant, and speaker - Temitope has worked with startups, enterprises, and international platforms to reimagine the future of work.
Her journey to building Outnovately AI was shaped by witnessing the same challenges repeatedly: HR professionals spending hours on document creation while job seekers struggled with ATS systems and interview preparation. She saw the disconnect between what recruiters needed and what candidates provided.
"I realized we needed a platform that serves both sides of the hiring equation. When HR teams have better tools and candidates present themselves more effectively, everyone wins. That's the vision behind Outnovately AI - creating workplace success for all."
As founder of the University of Freelancing and creator of the 30 Days Schools for AI and HR-Tech, Temitope continues to democratize access to cutting-edge workplace technology and career development resources.
