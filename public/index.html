About Outnovately AI
Passionate Team. Award Winning Software.
Outnovately AI is an HR-Tech innovation studio building practical, AI-powered tools for HR professionals, career-driven individuals, and future-ready organizations. At the heart of Outnovately AI is a bold mission: to simplify the complexities of hiring, evaluation, and workforce development through intuitive, ethical, and human-centered AI. We don't build for the hype. We build for the humans behind HR.
Meet Our Founder
Outnovately AI was founded by Temitope Okeseeyin
A Top 1% Global HR-Tech Strategist, AI Consultant, and Workforce Development Expert. With 10+ years of experience sitting on both sides of the hiring table, as a recruiter, coach, consultant, and speaker, Temitope has worked with startups, enterprises, and international platforms to reimagine the future of work.
Her journey to building Outnovately was shaped by years of witnessing broken processes: poorly written job descriptions, biased candidate evaluations, inefficient hiring workflows, and overwhelming manual tasks that hold HR back. She didn't just spot the problems, she built the tools to solve them. Outnovately AI is her way of saying: "HR deserves better."
As part of her commitment to democratizing the future of work, Temitope is the founder of the University of Freelancing, offering 30 Days Schools for AI and HR-Tech, these are pioneering coaching programs aimed at introducing professionals to practical applications of AI in human resources and the workplace.
